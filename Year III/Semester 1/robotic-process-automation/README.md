# Robotic Process Automation
*Robotic Process Automation (RPA) — Year III, Semester 1*

Business-process automation with UiPath: workflows, data scraping, Excel/CSV automation and orchestration.

## Materials
- **Lectures/** — 7 course-note PDFs.
- **Labs/** — 55 files: UiPath projects (`.xaml`) for labs 3, 4, 6, 9 and 12, plus lab slide decks (`.pptx`) and sample data.
- **Exams/** — the grading sheet (`Barem.xlsx`) and an example RPA process.
